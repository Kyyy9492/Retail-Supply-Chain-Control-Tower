# Business Questions

## Fulfillment and Delivery

1. What is the overall late delivery rate?
2. Which states or regions have the longest delivery lead times?
3. Which product categories are most associated with late delivery?
4. Which sellers have high order volume but poor delivery performance?
5. Does late delivery correlate with lower customer review score?
6. How does freight cost vary by category, state, and seller?

## Seller / Supplier Performance

1. Which sellers are reliable core partners?
2. Which sellers create operational risk because of high volume and high late rate?
3. Which sellers have high freight cost or poor review performance?
4. How can seller performance be summarized in a scorecard?

## Demand Forecasting

1. Which SKU-store combinations have predictable demand?
2. Which forecasting method performs best against a moving-average baseline?
3. Which products have high forecast error and should receive manual review?

## Inventory Planning

1. Which SKUs are high-value and high-volatility?
2. Which SKUs require higher safety stock?
3. What reorder points should be recommended under a 95% service-level assumption?
4. How does a forecast-based replenishment policy compare with a baseline policy?
