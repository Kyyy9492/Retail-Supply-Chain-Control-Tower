## Recommendations

- Prioritize operational review for sellers with high order volume and above-average late delivery rates.
- Monitor high-freight and high-delay product categories separately in the dashboard.
- Use forecast-based replenishment for AX/AY items and higher safety stock for AZ items.
- Review CZ items for overstock risk and consider simplified replenishment rules.

## Limitations

- Public datasets do not include all operational variables such as warehouse capacity, actual inventory records, supplier contracts, or real holding costs.
- Inventory policy simulation uses assumptions for lead time, service level, holding cost, and stockout penalty.
- Forecasting results should be interpreted as a planning-support tool rather than a fully deployed production model.
