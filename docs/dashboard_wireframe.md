# Dashboard Wireframe

## Page 1 — Executive Overview

KPI cards:

- Total Orders
- Total Revenue
- Average Delivery Days
- Late Delivery Rate
- Average Freight Cost
- Average Review Score

Charts:

- Monthly order trend
- Revenue trend
- Late delivery rate trend
- Orders by customer state

Filters:

- Date range
- Customer state
- Product category
- Seller state

## Page 2 — Delivery Performance

Charts:

- Delivery days distribution
- Late rate by state
- Average delay days by state
- Actual vs. estimated delivery days
- Top delayed product categories

## Page 3 — Seller Scorecard

Charts:

- Seller volume vs. late rate scatter plot
- Seller performance table
- Seller score distribution
- Top operational-risk sellers

Suggested seller segments:

- High volume / low delay: reliable core sellers
- High volume / high delay: operational risk sellers
- Low volume / low delay: potential growth sellers
- Low volume / high delay: low-priority risk sellers

## Page 4 — Demand Forecasting

Charts:

- Actual vs. forecast demand
- Forecast error by category/store
- WAPE by model
- Demand trend by SKU/store

## Page 5 — Inventory Recommendations

Charts/tables:

- ABC/XYZ matrix
- Recommended safety stock table
- Recommended reorder point table
- Stockout rate comparison
- Inventory cost comparison
